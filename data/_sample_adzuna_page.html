    Description

    We are looking for a motivated IT Specialist CFC apprentice to join our Information Technology department. This apprenticeship offers a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience across multiple IT domains, including operations, infrastructure, customer service, and cybersecurity, while working alongside experienced professionals.

    What you will learn:

    • 1st Year:
      • Prepare and set up computers and devices for employees
      • Provide IT support and services to users
    • 2nd Year:
      • Help manage IT infrastructure
      • Learn how systems and networks work in a professional environment
    • 3rd Year:
      • One-year internship with ECS, focused on customer service and user support
    • 4th Year:
      • One-year internship in the Security Department
      • Learn the basics of IT security and best practices

    Your profile

    • You are comfortable with math
    • You have a good level of English (some meetings are held in English)
    • You enjoy working with people and communicating
    • You are curious, motivated, and passionate about IT
    • You want to learn, grow, and build a future in technology

    Please send us your complete application, including:

    • Cover letter
    • CV
    • School transcripts from the last three years
